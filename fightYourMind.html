Fight your Mind

"Fight your Mind" is an adventure and survival game where players navigate a maze, face manifestations of their deepest fears, and rely on the beam of their flashlight to survive. The game creates a tense and immersive experience, challenging players to use both strategy and courage as they traverse the labyrinth and confront various threats.

My work on "Fight your Mind" involved developing the AI for the enemies, ensuring they provided a challenging and unpredictable experience. Additionally, I programmed the flashlight mechanics, which are crucial for survival and gameplay progression. I was responsible for designing and implementing various game systems, including enemy behavior, maze navigation, and the flashlight's impact on gameplay, to create a cohesive and engaging experience for players.
