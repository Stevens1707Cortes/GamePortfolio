AstroBaba

"AstroBaba" invites players to transform by absorbing enemies and uncover new abilities to conquer puzzles and embark on thrilling adventures. This charming and innovative game offers a unique experience where players harness the power of transformation to tackle challenges and explore new abilities.

My work on "AstroBaba" primarily involved programming the core mechanics, including character movement, the absorption and transformation system, and the diverse abilities linked to each transformation. I also implemented character animations in Unity, ensuring smooth and responsive visual feedback. Additionally, I developed the game's UI and HUD, creating an intuitive and visually appealing interface that enhances the player's interaction and overall experience with the game.
